---
title: "Cambiar letra fondo en Xterm"
date: 2025-03-09T12:29:42-03:00
tags: ['linux']
---
Abrí `~/.Xdefaults` y agregá:

```bash
xterm*background:black
xterm*foreground:white
xterm*faceName:Monospace
xterm*faceSize:15

xterm*font:-*-fixed-*-*-*-*-20-*-*-*-*-*-*-*
```

Luego lo cargás con 
```bash
xrdb ~/.Xdefaults
```
